Lab 3: Build Your Personal Website

The goal of this lab was to create this site! We were supposed to design and build a website to hold our work for this class with a required landing page, labs landing page, and a page for each lab completed so far.

Approach

First, we were told to develop site maps to give us an idea of how we wanted the structure to be. Then, I used the site map to create a basic skeleton of the pages and referenced the rubric to ensure I was meeting all the requirements and had to proper repository structure. After that, I filled in the contents of the pages and added styling. I developed a landing page, labs landing page, and a page for each lab so far. I also made sure to push to GitHub and pull from the VM to check that the structure of my site was correct and that the pages were working as expected.
